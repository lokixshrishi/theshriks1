AI Automation Services

AI automation for operations, internal tools, and production workflows

The Shriks designs AI automation systems for founders and teams that want real operational leverage instead of demo-only automation. We work on workflow automation, AI-assisted backoffice systems, internal tools, and production integrations that reduce repetitive work without sacrificing reliability.

What we build

  • Workflow automation for repeated internal business processes
  • AI-assisted internal tools for support, research, routing, and operations
  • Automation layers that connect APIs, databases, and existing business systems
  • Operational dashboards and control surfaces for human-in-the-loop workflows

Who it fits

This work fits startups and teams that already know where their process bottlenecks are. If the same manual decisions, repetitive tasks, or cross-tool workflows keep slowing the business down, AI automation becomes useful when it is attached to the real production flow rather than isolated in a proof of concept.

How we approach it

We prioritize correctness, observability, and long-term maintainability. That means clear integrations, fallback paths, good monitoring, and interfaces that make sense for the operators using the system daily.
