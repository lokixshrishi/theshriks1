Systems Architecture Services

Systems architecture for products that need to stay stable after launch

The Shriks works on backend architecture, event-driven systems, system integration, and infrastructure planning for products that need more than quick delivery. We design systems for long-term operation, production load, and engineering clarity.

Common architecture work

  • Backend system design and service boundaries
  • Event-driven systems and stream-based workflows
  • Data flow planning across APIs, databases, and internal services
  • Production architecture reviews for scale, failure handling, and maintainability

Where this matters

This is useful when a product is outgrowing ad hoc engineering choices, when multiple services are starting to interact in fragile ways, or when the system needs clearer patterns for deployment, data ownership, and operational visibility.

What good architecture buys you

Cleaner product velocity, fewer expensive rewrites, safer releases, and a much more understandable codebase for the engineers who need to maintain it. The goal is not architecture theatre. The goal is a system that survives growth.
