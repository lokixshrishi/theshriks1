What's Kn8t?

Kn8t is our in-house automation platform built to design, run, and scale complex workflows across products, services, and systems. Originally developed to power our own internal operations, Kn8t enables developer-grade automations with business-level clarity—connecting APIs, tools, AI models, and logic into reliable, production-ready flows.
Designed for both developers and growing businesses, Kn8t helps teams replace manual processes and brittle scripts with structured, intelligent automation that evolves as systems scale.

System-Level Product Building:

We build production-grade applications across web, mobile, and platform environments, focusing on reliability, clarity, and long-term maintainability. Rather than treating applications as isolated interfaces, we design them as part of larger systems—connected to data foundations, automation, and operational workflows.

Our work covers end-to-end product development, from system design and backend services to user-facing interfaces. Whether the product runs in a browser, on mobile devices, or as a platform service, we prioritize correctness, performance, and behavior after deployment, not just how it looks at launch.

OF THE SHRIKS
About

We of THE SHRIKS design and build independent systems across modern computing domains. Our work focuses on long-running, production-grade software—systems that are meant to operate reliably beyond initial deployment. We work across intelligence, distributed systems, data foundations, and software infrastructure, with an emphasis on clarity, autonomy, and reducing reliance on monolithic technology platforms.



THE SHRIKS is led by Laukik and Shrusti, working together behind the studio to shape its direction and systems. Alongside collaborative projects, we develop our own foundational tools and platforms, built from first principles and real operational constraints. The studio exists to create smaller, sharper, and more deliberate systems that can stand on their own over time.
